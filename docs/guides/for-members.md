# Tahti for cooperative members

You are a **member** of Tahti ry if you pay the **€40/year** membership (or were activated in dev). Members can use **governance** and, if you also run a channel, the full **artist studio**.

This guide is **not** about paying an artist monthly (fan subscription) — see [For viewers](for-viewers.md).

---

## 1. Join and pay membership

1. **Sign up:** `/join` → check email → open `/verify?token=…`
2. **Log in:** `/login`
3. **Dashboard:** `/dashboard` → **Tahti ry membership** → pay or activate (Stripe when enabled)

When active, your account shows a **member number** and `Member governance` links on the dashboard.

---

## 2. Governance

1. Open **`/governance`** (members only — you must be logged in).
2. **Member directory** — who is in the cooperative (public names and member numbers).
3. **Motions** — proposals from the board or members; vote while a motion is **open**.
4. Votes are **advisory** in year one; binding AGM rules are documented separately.

If you see “sign in required”, log in with the same account you used for membership.

---
